# Your Privacy on SourcePath

**Short summary** — full technical policy: `privacy-data-retention-policy.md`

---

## What SourcePath is

SourcePath helps you find **immigration legal information** from official government sources. It is **not** a law firm and does not give legal advice.

---

## What we keep private by design

- **We do not save your full questions or answers on our servers** after you get a response.
- **We do not keep a chat history** on the server for you to log back into later.
- On your phone, your current conversation stays **in the app session** until you leave or reload—it is not saved as a chat log in cloud storage.

---

## What we might store (limited)

To improve safety and reliability, we may store **non-identifying** information, such as:

- A scrambled fingerprint (hash) of your question—not the full wording
- Which official sources were cited
- Whether the app refused a risky request
- How long the answer took to generate

We use this to monitor quality, **not** to read your personal story.

---

## Guest mode

If you use the app without an account:

- We only remember how many questions you have asked as a guest (on your device), so we can show a fair usage limit.
- We do **not** store your name or email for guest mode.

---

## Accounts (coming later)

When sign-in is available, we will store account information needed to log you in (such as email and a secure password). We still **will not** store your chat messages on the server.

---

## Where answers come from

Answers are built from **public legal sources** (regulations, statutes, USCIS policy) using AI that runs **locally or on our private servers**—not sent to public chatbot websites for training.

---

## Your choices

- Use **guest mode** for fewer stored identifiers.
- **Clear app data** on your phone to reset guest usage and onboarding.
- When accounts exist, you may **delete your account**; there will be no server chat history to export because we do not keep it.

---

## Questions

Contact the operator listed in the app **About** screen or website privacy page before launch.
